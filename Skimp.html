Skimp

Financial Management App

Overview

When you're young and have few responsibilities, it can be difficult to keep track of your finances. With proper guidance and advice, many people can save a large amount of money and invest for their future.

Solution

Students or young people can benefit from an app that promotes saving for the future to avoid getting in debt or spending more than they can afford. Additionally, chatting with a financial expert and receiving financial tips can help users make better financial decisions. The app is called Skimp+, and it means to spend less.

Challenge

The purpose of this project is to design iOS and Android versions of a native mobile application that tracks income and expenses, sets goals, motivates and teaches users to save.

Targeted Users:

Parents, Patients and Care Workers

The PHLOC App is unique because it enables users to look for medications before visiting a pharmacy. This is not possible with most apps, unless the pharmacy is an online retailer.

Rapid Prototyping

The rapid prototyping method Crazy 8 was used to sketch ideas based on the user flow. These 8 sketches show the basic functionality of logging in, searching for medications, and finding a location. Using Invision, I developed a prototype and tested it on three targeted users for feedback

Design

Prototype

Recruited three users and gave them four simple tasks to complete while I observed as they attempted to complete the task. Noted down the issues and reiterated my designs to enable the user meet their goals.
