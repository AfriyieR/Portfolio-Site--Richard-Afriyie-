About Me

My story

I am a UI designer with 8+ years of graphic design experience. A graduate of the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), Kumasi-Ghana with a degree in Communication Design. While working as a graphic designer, I helped develop 20+ design projects (logos, brochures, ads) that increased the brand awareness of the companies.

In addition, I co-founded a small design company where I developed leadership, organisational, and management skills.   

Having relocated to the UK, I worked as a remote intern for a company called Pharmhedge in the USA. The project was an online medical marketplace called Unilever. Working as a UI/UX designer for the first time gave me insight into what user experience design and user interface design were all about.

My role was to use a design system to transform low fidelity wireframes into high fidelity wireframes. I collaborated with UX designers, UI designers, Product designers, Developers and Project Managers. I decided to pursue this career path further by enrolling in an online UI immersion programme with Career Foundry to get a structured and better understanding of UI/UX design. I am seeking to work for a FinTech company as a UI designer. This will enable me to impact lives positively and help solve complex problems with simple, clean and easy to navigate solutions.
