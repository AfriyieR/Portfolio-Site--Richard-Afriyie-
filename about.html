About Me

My story

I am a UI designer with a background in graphic design. Working as a remote intern on a medical marketplace called Unirever as a UI/UX designer gave me experience in this area. My role was to use a design system to transform low fidelity wireframes into high fidelity wireframes.

I collaborated with UX designers, UI designers, Product designers, Developers and Project Managers. The feedback I got from the internship encouraged me to pursue UI design further.

I enrolled on a UI immersion programme with Career Foundary to get a structured and better understanding of UI/UX design. While working in graphic design, I helped develop 20+ design projects (logos, brochures, ads) that increased the brand awareness and transactional level of the company.

In addition,I co-founded a small design company where I developed leadership, organisational, and management skills.   I am seeking to work for FinTech Company as UI designer to impact lives positively with my skills.
